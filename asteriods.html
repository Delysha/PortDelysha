The rift Guardian

What is this project about?

The Rift Guardian is about a person who went through a portal to a strange planet filled with portals. Suddenly, the person is attacked by aliens coming through the portals. Now, the person's mission is to protect the portal leading to the human world. The game is a VR shooter made in Unreal Engine 5.3 with Blueprints.

Project date: From February 2024 - june 2024

Project duration: 4 months.

Development Team: 24 people.

Engine: Unreal engine 5.3

Programming language: Blueprints
